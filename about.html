About Us

Who Are We?

We are Centennial High School’s FTC Robotics Team, Robot In Pieces (Team 20343), dedicated to cultivating student interest in STEM. Our mission is to prepare members for real-world experiences in robotics, programming, engineering, and marketing — all while fostering creativity and teamwork.

What Do We Do?

Our team builds competitive robots for the FIRST Tech Challenge and participates in regional tournaments. Members gain hands-on experience in hardware design, software development, and strategic thinking. We also manage outreach initiatives, build professional connections, and document our engineering process.

Why FTC?

FTC teaches more than just robotics. It instills leadership, collaboration, and technical fluency in students of all skill levels. Our team values Gracious Professionalism™, perseverance through failure, and growth through mentorship. FTC offers every student a pathway into STEM and builds confidence through exploration.

"Everyone starts somewhere — no one learns everything all at once. Through challenges, failures, and teamwork, we've grown stronger, more skilled, and more united."

STEM Education

We promote STEM to our local community through workshops, camps, and mentoring opportunities.

Leadership & Growth

Every member is given chances to lead initiatives and contribute their strengths to the team.

Outreach & Impact

Our outreach team collaborates with local businesses and schools to expand access to robotics education.
